        Transparency & Verifiability

        • Published before outcomes are known. Trade signals are committed to this repository when they are issued, before any result exists.
        • Continuously updated. CSV files are updated over time as trades progress, recording daily maximum profit and loss until every trade closes.
        • Git commit history is the audit trail. Every change to every CSV is permanently recorded with a timestamp.
        • Historical changes are publicly visible. Anyone can inspect the full revision history of any signal file.
        • Verifiable performance. All analytics on this site are computed live in your browser from the raw CSV files, with nothing pre-aggregated or hidden.
